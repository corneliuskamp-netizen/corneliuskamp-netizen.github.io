Why I started Supporting Redbull Racing

I started supporting the Red Bull Racing Formula 1 team in 2022, primarily because of their lead driver, Max Verstappen. My support for Max stems from several reasons, the main one being that he is Dutch, which reflects one of my nationalities. Beyond that, he is an exceptionally talented and experienced driver with four World Drivers' Championships to his name. I also admire him for his personality, his ability to ignore outside criticism, and his perseverance in making the most of any situation he finds himself in. They are having a rough start this year, but I believe we can bounce back in 2026.
