Avengers: Infinity War Characters

Thanos tries to collect all six Infinity Stones to wipe out half of all life. The Avengers and Guardians of the Galaxy team up to stop him. Despite fighting hard on different planets, they fail to keep the stones from Thanos. At the end, Thanos snaps his fingers, killing half of all life and leaving the surviving heroes broken and defeated.

I like the movie Avengers: Infinity War because it has a lot of action and a good story. The advanced CGI and special effect are really cool. I also like how it connects to the other Marvel movies and sets up for the next one, Avengers: Endgame.

  • Captain America - First appeared in Captain America: The First Avenger
  • Iron Man - One of the few characters without superpowers
  • Doctor Strange - Former neurosurgeon who became Sorcerer Supreme
  • Spider-Man - Youngest Avenger in Infinity War
