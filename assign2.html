The Avengers

Directed by Joss Whedon, the superhero film "The Avengers" was released in 2012 and produced by Marvel Studios.

After Loki steals the Tesseract and starts an alien attack, Nick Fury brings together a team of heroes called the Avengers to save Earth. They don't get along at first, but the death of Agent Coulson helps them put aside their differences. During a big battle in New York City, they work as a team to stop the alien army and close the portal. At the end, Iron Man risks his life by carrying a missile through the portal to destroy the enemy ship, then falls back to Earth and survives. The Avengers capture Loki.

Avengers: Infinity War

Directed by Anthony Russo and Joe Russo, "Avengers: Infinity War" was released in 2018 and produced by Marvel Studios.

>Thanos tries to collect all six Infinity Stones to wipe out half of all life. The Avengers and Guardians of the Galaxy team up to stop him. Despite fighting hard on different planets, they fail to keep the stones from Thanos. At the end, Thanos snaps his fingers, killing half of all life and leaving the surviving heroes broken and defeated.
