2021 Formula 1 Season

The 2021 season featured an intense championship battle between Max Verstappen and Lewis Hamilton. Verstappen of Red Bull Racing-Honda won his first World Drivers' Championship with 10 race wins, finishing 8 points ahead of defending champion Hamilton of Mercedes. Mercedes won the Constructors' Championship for the eighth consecutive season. The two title rivals exchanged the lead multiple times and were involved in major collisions at the British and Italian Grands Prix. Both drivers entered the final race in Abu Dhabi tied on points, where a controversial late safety car restart allowed Verstappen to overtake Hamilton on the last lap and claim the title. The controversy led to the removal of race director Michael Masi and structural changes to race control.
